# Moat and defensibility

Potential moats:
- Food operations workflow depth.
- Production/packing/order data model.
- Integrations and product mapping.
- Implementation playbooks.
- Customer switching costs after menus, recipes, staff, labels, and reports are configured.
- Partner network.
- Reporting layer across brands/locations.

Risks:
- POS platforms could expand into production.
- Shopify apps could copy narrow workflows.
- Restaurants may resist new software.
- Integrations require maintenance and marketplace approvals.

Defensive strategy: win the operational layer after checkout, not generic commerce or POS.
