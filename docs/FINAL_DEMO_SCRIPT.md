# Final customer demo script (~15 minutes)

## Discovery questions (2 min)

1. Where do orders appear today before the kitchen sees them?
2. Last time a channel dropped — what happened?
3. Who owns integrations vs line execution?

## Talk track + screens

### 1. Problem (30s)

“Spreadsheets and tablets don’t agree — we centralize **incoming** orders before production commits.”

### 2. Multi-channel chaos (1 min)

Landing integrations hub → honest **live vs simulated** posture.

### 3. Order Hub (3 min)

`/dashboard/order-hub` — unified queue, channel badges, drill-in.

### 4. Product matching (1 min)

Products/menus — how SKUs map for sync (conceptual).

### 5. Production planning (2 min)

`/dashboard/production` — batches and checkpoints.

### 6. Kitchen screen (1 min)

`/dashboard/kitchen` — execution-focused view.

### 7. Packing & labels (2 min)

`/dashboard/packing` — printable workflow.

### 8. Analytics (1 min)

`/dashboard/analytics` — channel mix / throughput snapshot.

### 9. Integrations (2 min)

`/dashboard/integrations` — webhook URLs, test tools, logs.

### 10. Pricing (1 min)

`/pricing` or `/dashboard/billing` — Starter vs Pro vs Team; Enterprise contact.

### 11. Next step (30s)

“If we solve Order Hub + production visibility first, when can your Shopify/Woo dev join for webhooks?”

## Close question

“Does a 14-day pilot with weekly check-ins fit how you buy software?”

> Thanks for walking through OS Kitchen today.
