# Staff training guide

Routes:
- `/dashboard/training`
- `/dashboard/training/kitchen`
- `/dashboard/training/packing`
- `/dashboard/training/manager`

Training principles:
- Use practice language and test orders during onboarding.
- Do not ask staff to mutate live data until a manager confirms.
- Train by role: kitchen, packing, manager, owner.

Modules:
- Kitchen: production board, quantities, cooked/packed/labeled flags.
- Packing: verification, labels, mismatches.
- Manager: order hub, menu planner, reports, integrations.
- Owner: billing, analytics, go-live, support escalation.
