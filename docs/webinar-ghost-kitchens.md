## Story arc (2 minutes)

### 1 — The pain (25s)

Three brands, five tablets, two spreadsheet P&Ls. DoorDash tickets in one app, Shopify preorders in another, walk-in counter sales in a third. Kitchen staff can't tell which brand a ticket belongs to until someone shouts it. Margin by brand? Export Friday, fix Monday.

### 2 — The wedge (30s)

OS Kitchen is **one tenant graph** for multi-brand ops: Order Hub normalizes every channel, KDS filters by source when you need it, Today Command Center surfaces cross-brand blockers. Integration Health shows PASS, BETA, or SKIPPED — not fake green tiles. Software-first POS on hardware you already own.

### 3 — What works today (35s)

| Surface | Route | Ghost-kitchen hook |
|---------|-------|-------------------|
| **Today Command Center** | `/dashboard/today` | Cross-brand briefing + attention strip |
| **Order Hub** | `/dashboard/orders` | Unified queue · filter by channel/brand |
| **KDS** | `/dashboard/kitchen` | Shared line · source badges |
| **Multi-brand analytics** | `/dashboard/analytics` | Brand P&L snapshot scaffold |
| **Integration Health** | `/dashboard/integration-health` | Honest channel maturity |

**Say explicitly:** Uber Eats / DoorDash adapters are **roadmap or BETA** per registry — we do not claim official partner approval on this webinar.

### 4 — Pilot offer (20s)

Three-month ghost kitchen design partner LOI: staging workspace, weekly 30-minute feedback, influence on multi-brand rollup priority. Pilot pricing per [`/pricing`](../app/pricing/page.tsx) — 50% subscription discount during qualified window.

### 5 — Honest close (10s)

We're recruiting **founding ghost kitchen operators** to prove multi-brand order→kitchen in real service — not claiming Deliverect parity or certified aggregator sync. Book a fit call; we screen-share Integration Health with SKIPPED rows visible.
