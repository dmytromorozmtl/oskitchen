# OS Kitchen positioning

> **Built for operators who need more than Square but can't afford Toast's hardware lock-in.**

OS Kitchen is the **operations layer after the sale**. It does **not** replace Shopify, WooCommerce, Uber Eats, or POS systems — it centralizes production planning, packing, routing, and inventory truth once orders exist.

See also: [`docs/positioning-reformulation.md`](positioning-reformulation.md) (Blueprint P1-72).

## Narrative pillars

1. **Channel-agnostic kitchen OS** — ingest from many carts, execute once.
2. **Margin-aware fulfillment** — costing + forecasting tie revenue to ingredients.
3. **Operator-grade tooling** — labels, routes, staff tasks, CRM context.

## Messaging guardrails

- Never claim marketplace “approval” until badges exist.
- Call Uber modules **architecture + credential gated**.
- Highlight exports + API for enterprise trust.
