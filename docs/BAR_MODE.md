# Bar mode

**Focus:** Drinks-led menu, costing / pour discipline, events, inventory, purchasing, staff tasks.

**Recommended modules:** Menus, products, costing, ingredient demand, purchasing, tasks, catering (for private events), reports.

**Terminology:** “Drinks & events menu”, “Bar prep”, “Orders & requests”.

**Compliance:** Do **not** claim alcohol delivery legality in-app without jurisdiction-specific configuration and legal review. Nutrition for alcohol remains optional; disclaimers belong in `Nutrition labels` and customer-facing copy.

**Gaps / next sprint themes:** Happy hour windows as structured entities; pour-cost wizard tied to bottle inventory.
