## pm-gtm-icp-profile

### Ideal buyer

| Attribute | Fit |
|-----------|-----|
| Model | Independent restaurant or small group wanting commission-free direct ordering with branded web presence |
| Pain | Third-party marketplace fees; wants own channel with logo, theme color, and catering landing pages |
| Stack | OS Kitchen storefront + PWA; DNS access at registrar for custom domain (manual setup) |
| Disqualifier | Requires instant DNS automation, white-label App Store binaries, or guaranteed custom domain SLA |

### Sales-safe wedge

> “**ChowNow parity** depth dashboard — **BETA** maturity labels per capability. **DNS is not automatic** — **Do not promise custom domains** until verified at registrar.”

**Pilot wedge:** Single-brand operator — T+1 upload logo + brand color, T+3 publish storefront slug, T+7 walk custom domain checklist with founder.

## pm-gtm-objection-handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “Do you set up our custom domain?” | **DNS is not automatic** — we provide checklist; customer configures registrar. **Do not promise custom domains** in contract until verified. |
| “ChowNow ships branded apps.” | **ChowNow parity** is our benchmark — App Store binaries may be **SKIPPED** or **ROADMAP**; web PWA is **BETA**. |
| “Is commission-free really free?” | Direct ordering avoids marketplace commission — payment processing fees still apply. |
| “Can you hide OS Kitchen branding?” | Enterprise hide-branding is partner-gated — label **SKIPPED** until contract scope confirmed. |

## pm-gtm-pricing-talk-track

- **Standard pilot:** White-label storefront depth included — no separate ChowNow-parity SKU on [`/pricing`](/pricing).
- **Custom domain setup:** DNS checklist + founder assist — not bundled as “instant domain provisioning.”
- **Enterprise white-label App Store:** Custom quote or decline — never list guaranteed App Store binaries in standard pricing.
- Anchor **sales-safe** claims until `/trust` updates storefront language.

## pm-gtm-honesty-guardrails

**Do **not** claim:**

- Automatic DNS provisioning or guaranteed custom domain go-live dates  
- White-label iOS/Android App Store binaries as standard pilot deliverable  
- Zero payment processing fees on “commission-free” direct ordering  
- Full **ChowNow parity** without **BETA** / **SKIPPED** maturity labels  

**Always label:** **ChowNow parity** · **BETA** · **SKIPPED** where blocked · **DNS is not automatic** · **Do not promise custom domains** · **sales-safe** talk tracks only  

**Human gate:** Founder + storefront ops sign-off before any enterprise slide stating custom domain or App Store scope.
