# Investor narrative

Problem: food businesses sell through modern channels but fulfill with spreadsheets.

Market: meal prep, catering, bakeries, ghost kitchens, and preorder food operators.

Wedge: operations after orders arrive: order hub, production, packing, routing, imports, implementation.

Why now: small food brands are more multi-channel and need operational maturity earlier.

GTM: beta customers, implementation packages, agency partners, Shopify/Woo ecosystem.

Risks: compliance review, integration approval, SMB churn, operational support load.

Traction placeholders: collect signups, beta leads, demos, activated accounts, paid conversions, retention, support volume.
