# Accounting / GL Roadmap

## Current State

KitchenOS has accounting-adjacent operations, but should not claim a full native
general ledger today.

Existing strengths:

- AP-adjacent invoice workflows
- purchasing support
- finance/reporting surfaces
- billing/monetization data

## Gaps

- chart of accounts
- double-entry GL
- daily sales journal
- payroll journal
- month-end close workflows
- strong AR/AP ownership at full accounting depth

## Recommended Build Order

1. daily sales journal
2. vendor invoice normalization
3. chart-of-accounts foundation
4. AP basics
5. GL posting architecture

## Positioning Rule

Until GL posting and journal ownership are real, market this area as:

- finance ops
- purchasing / AP-adjacent
- accounting integrations and reporting

Not as:

- full native accounting platform
