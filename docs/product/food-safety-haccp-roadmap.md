# Food Safety / HACCP Roadmap

## Current State

Existing surface:

- `app/dashboard/food-safety/page.tsx`
- related checklist / temperature / audit flows

Current truth:

- the module exists
- it is not yet a complete enterprise HACCP compliance suite

## Near-Term Scope

1. temperature logs
2. HACCP checklists
3. line checks
4. corrective actions
5. alerting for out-of-range readings
6. audit exports

## Recommended Status

- `PILOT_ONLY` until corrective actions, alerts, exports, and audit evidence are stronger

## Competitive Gap

KitchenOS trails Xenia and CrunchTime in compliance depth, but already has a
better chance to connect food-safety workflows to production, packing, and
operator dashboards in one system.
