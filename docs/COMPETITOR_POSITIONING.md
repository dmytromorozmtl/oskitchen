# Competitor positioning

> **vs Toast:** Hardware shouldn't lock you in. See [`docs/toast-positioning.md`](toast-positioning.md).

> **vs Square:** Enterprise features without enterprise contracts. See [`docs/square-positioning.md`](square-positioning.md).

> **vs Lightspeed:** Built for food operators. See [`docs/lightspeed-positioning.md`](lightspeed-positioning.md).

> **vs MarketMan:** Full OS — including marketplace. See [`docs/marketman-positioning.md`](marketman-positioning.md).

> **vs MarginEdge:** Invoice AI is a feature, not a product. See [`docs/marginedge-positioning.md`](marginedge-positioning.md).

OS Kitchen does not replace sales channels. It centralizes operations after orders arrive.

Against spreadsheets: fewer manual counts, better audit trail, safer imports.

Against Shopify/WooCommerce alone: sales channels sell; OS Kitchen produces, packs, routes, and reports.

Against restaurant POS: OS Kitchen focuses on preorder, production, and fulfillment workflows.

Against catering suites: OS Kitchen includes imports, mapping, production, packing, and multi-channel order hub.

Against delivery platforms: OS Kitchen coordinates execution across channels; it does not own demand.
