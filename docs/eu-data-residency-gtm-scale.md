## pm-gtm-icp-profile

| Attribute | Fit |
|-----------|-----|
| Geography | EU/UK HQ or processing EU customer PII at scale |
| Trigger | RFP clause requiring EU-only storage or DPA with residency appendix |

## pm-gtm-objection-handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “Is our data stored in the EU?” | **Not available** for EU-dedicated production region today — **US-primary** hosting. SCCs via subprocessors; DPA review required. |
| “Are you GDPR certified?” | We are **not GDPR certified** as a product marketing claim. Operator-dependent compliance; legal review before RFP answers. |
| “Toast/Square host in EU.” | Competitors vary — our wedge is honest roadmap + pilot design-partner path, not false residency claims. |
| “We need EU-only before signing.” | Escalate to founder + legal — Phase 3 pilot SOW only after Phase 1–2 gates; do not verbal-commit GA date. |

## pm-gtm-honesty-guardrails

**Do **not** claim:**

- EU data residency or “data stays in the EU” for production tenants today  
- GDPR certification or SOC2-equivalent residency certification  
- Fixed GA date for EU region before Phase 5 exit criteria  
- That all subprocessors store data exclusively in the EU  

**Always label:** **US-primary** · **Not available** (EU-dedicated region) · **~15%** roadmap readiness · **not GDPR certified** · **sales-safe** talk tracks only  

**Human gate:** Founder + legal sign-off before any enterprise slide or contract appendix stating EU residency.
