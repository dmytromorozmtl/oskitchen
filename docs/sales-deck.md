## Slide 2: Problem

- Kitchens lose **3–8% revenue** on food waste and rework
- Orders from **multiple channels** (walk-in, preorder, delivery apps) create chaos
- Production planning is **manual** — spreadsheets, whiteboards, WhatsApp
- Packing mistakes cost **time, refunds, and reputation**
- Legacy POS vendors lock you into **hardware leases** ($2K–5K upfront)

## Slide 3: Solution

**One platform:** POS + Storefront + Production + Packing + Delivery

KitchenOS connects front-of-house and back-of-house in real time — from the moment an order arrives until it leaves the door.

## Slide 6: Core Modules

- **POS Terminal** — counter checkout, cash + Stripe, handheld mode
- **Online Storefront** — branded preorder site, Stripe checkout, QR menus
- **Production Board** — batch planning, yield, station routing
- **Packing & Verification** — item checklist, labels, waves
- **Kitchen Display (KDS)** — real-time order queue, Supabase Realtime
- **Inventory & Costing** — food cost, margin rules, purchasing demand
