# 10-minute sales demo script

**Audience:** Owner/GM of meal prep, catering, ghost kitchen, or bakery.  
**Goal:** Show centralized orders → production → packing → analytics → integrations → pricing → next step.

## Before the call

- Open `/demo/meal-prep` (or vertical matching prospect).
- Confirm **demo banner** visible; integrations labeled **simulated** where applicable.
- Hide unrelated browser tabs; test screen share audio/video.

## Minute 0–1 — Business pain

“We’re seeing teams lose orders between Shopify, WooCommerce, spreadsheets, and Uber tablets — production guesses quantities and packing mislabels bags.”

## Minute 1–3 — Order Hub

Navigate: **Order Hub / Orders**.

- Show unified queue, filters, channel badges.
- Drill into one order — emphasize **sanitized customer view** on public flows.

## Minute 3–4 — Connected channels

**Integrations** page:

- Explain **Live vs simulated** states.
- Copy webhook URL example for their engineer.

## Minute 4–6 — Production

Open **Production / kitchen board**.

- Walk checkpoints (prep → cook → pack).
- Mention staff roles on Team tier.

## Minute 5–6 — Packing & labels

**Packing** — generate PDF (lazy-loaded) — show label-oriented workflow.

## Minute 6–7 — Analytics

**Analytics** — revenue/channel snapshot grounded in DB.

## Minute 7–8 — Integrations depth

Open marketing **`/integrations`** hub if discussing roadmap credibility.

## Minute 8–9 — Pricing

Landing **Pricing** — Starter vs Pro vs Team vs Enterprise contact.

## Minute 9–10 — Next step CTA

“Start a free trial with your workspace, or join the **beta** for white-glove onboarding.”  
Buttons: **Book demo** (mailto or calendar) / **Start free trial** (`/signup`).

## Guided tour (optional)

Add lightweight tooltips in demo routes pointing to: Orders → Production → Packing → Analytics → Integrations.
