# Beta email sequences (manual send)

**Rules:** Do not send mass mail automatically from this document. Use your ESP (e.g. Resend) from **`/dashboard/growth`** actions only when explicitly triggered. Personalize every send.

---

## 1. Beta application received

**Trigger:** `BetaLead` created  
**Subject:** We received your KitchenOS beta application  
**Body:**  
Hi {Name},  
Thanks for applying — we read every submission. We’re onboarding food businesses in cohorts so we can stay hands-on.  
Quick asks:  
- Confirm your primary channel ({channels})  
- Weekly order band you selected  
We’ll follow up with next steps or a short fit call if it’s a mutual match.

---

## 2. Demo booked

**Trigger:** Demo moved to SCHEDULED (manual or calendar)  
**Subject:** KitchenOS demo — what to prep  
**Body:**  
Hi {Name},  
Looking forward to our demo. Bring:  
- Rough weekly order volume  
- Current stack (POS / marketplace / ecommerce)  
- The last operational fire drill you’d eliminate if you could  
We’ll walk Order Hub → Production → Packing with your scenario.

---

## 3. Demo follow-up

**Trigger:** Demo COMPLETED  
**Subject:** Recap + suggested pilot scope  
**Body:**  
Hi {Name},  
Thanks for the time today. Here’s what I heard:  
- Pain: {pain}  
- Channels: {channels}  
Suggested pilot: {2–3 bullets}  
Next step: {call / sandbox invite / beta slot}.

---

## 4. Onboarding invitation

**Trigger:** Beta approved  
**Subject:** You’re invited — KitchenOS beta cohort  
**Body:**  
Hi {Name},  
Welcome to the KitchenOS beta. Your workspace is ready at {url}.  
Week-one goal: complete one full operational cycle (menu → order → production → packing).  
I’m your direct line for feedback — reply anytime.

---

## 5. Activation reminder

**Trigger:** Account idle 3–5 days; checklist incomplete  
**Subject:** Quick win inside KitchenOS  
**Body:**  
Hi {Name},  
The fastest “aha” is running one test order through production and packing. If anything feels blocking, tell me which step — we’ll unblock it.

## 6. Feedback request

**Trigger:** 10+ active days or post-milestone  
**Subject:** What should we build next?  
**Body:**

What’s the one workflow still costing you time each week?  
Hit reply or use the in-app Feedback button — it captures your page context.
