# Buyer personas

- **Meal prep owner:** wants weekly menu planning, packing accuracy, and pickup/delivery control.
- **Chef-owner:** wants less admin and clearer production quantities.
- **Catering manager:** wants quotes to become production, packing, and route plans.
- **Ghost kitchen operator:** wants multi-channel order visibility and product mapping.
- **Operations manager:** wants staff tasks, reports, and fewer launch-day surprises.
- **Bakery owner:** wants preorder cutoffs, bake schedules, labels, and pickup queues.
