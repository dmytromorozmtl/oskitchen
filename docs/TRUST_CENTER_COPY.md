# Trust center copy

OS Kitchen is designed with security-conscious practices for B2B food operations.

Use approved wording:
- Stripe handles payment processing.
- Customer data export tools are available.
- Formal compliance certifications are future roadmap items.
- Integrations require customer-provided credentials and provider access.
- AI assistance is not a substitute for legal, tax, nutrition, allergen, or operational review.

Avoid:
- SOC 2 compliant
- HIPAA compliant
- PCI compliant
- GDPR compliant
- Certified food labeling compliance
- Official Shopify/WooCommerce/Uber approval
