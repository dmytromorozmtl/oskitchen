# System workspace templates

The 7 starters shipped with KitchenOS today. See
`docs/TEMPLATE_REGISTRY.md` for the canonical table.

## Restaurant starter
- Modules: Orders, Menus, Production, Tasks, Purchasing, Costing, Analytics.
- Playbook: Restaurant Daily Prep.
- Setup tasks: 5 (menu, integrations, costing, staff, go-live).

## Café starter
- Modules: Menus, Storefront, Production, Tasks, Purchasing, CRM.
- Playbook: Café Morning Setup.
- Setup tasks: 5.

## Bar starter
- Modules: Menus, Catering quotes, Costing, Tasks, Calendar.
- Playbook: Bar Event Night.
- Setup tasks: 5. No compliance claims.

## Bakery starter
- Modules: Menus, Storefront, Production, Packing, Nutrition labels, Routes.
- Playbook: Bakery Preorder Day.
- Setup tasks: 5.

## Catering starter
- Modules: Catering quotes, CRM, Calendar, Production, Packing, Routes, Reports.
- Playbook: Catering Event Workflow.
- Suggested packages: Corporate Lunch, Buffet, Boxed Meals.

## Meal prep starter
- Modules: Menus, Meal plans, Production, Packing, Nutrition labels, Routes, CRM.
- Playbook: Meal Prep Weekly Cycle.
- Setup tasks: 5.

## Ghost kitchen starter
- Modules: Brands, Order hub, Sales channels, Production, Packing verify, Analytics.
- Playbook: Ghost Kitchen Rush.
- Multi-brand-ready; recommended after at least one Brand exists.
