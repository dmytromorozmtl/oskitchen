# OS Kitchen support SLA (pilots & paid)

| Plan | Channels | Standard response | P1 (production down) |
|------|----------|-------------------|----------------------|
| Starter ($29) | Email | 48h business days | Best effort |
| Pro ($79) | Email + in-app chat | 24h | 8h |

**Knowledge base:** `/dashboard/support/kb` and contextual `?` help links in dashboard modules.
