# Claims review

Unsafe claims scanned and avoided:
- SOC 2 / HIPAA / PCI / GDPR compliance
- Food labeling or nutrition compliance
- Tax compliance
- Official Shopify/WooCommerce/Uber approval
- AI accuracy guarantees
- Delivery availability guarantees
- Revenue or ROI guarantees
- Real customer traction without proof

Approved wording:
- Designed with security-conscious practices.
- Stripe handles payment processing.
- Customer data export tools are available.
- Uber Eats requires appropriate partner/API access.
- Demo scenarios are clearly labeled.
- ROI calculator is an estimate, not a guarantee.
