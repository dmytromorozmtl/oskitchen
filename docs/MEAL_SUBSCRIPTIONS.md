# Meal subscriptions

## What it does

Tracks **`CustomerSubscription`** plans tied to **`KitchenCustomer`** emails with cadence, fulfillment preference, notes, and pause/resume/cancel controls.

## Setup

Dashboard → **Meal subscriptions** → capture guest email + plan metadata.

## Limitations

- Auto-generation of downstream orders is **manual** for now — dates act as reminders.

## Future improvements

- Cron-generated picks tied to active menus.
- Self-service guest portal.
