# Staff & tasks

## What it does

Maintains a **`StaffMember`** roster and **`KitchenTask`** checklist with types (`PREP`, `COOK`, `PACK`, etc.), optional assignees, due timestamps, and basic status transitions surfaced on `/dashboard/tasks` and `/dashboard/staff`.

## Setup

Add teammates under **Staff**, then create tasks and assign as needed.

## Limitations

- No authentication mapping per staff row yet (tasks are workspace-level).
- Role visibility filters described in the roadmap remain TODO.

## Future improvements

- Kanban columns + mobile assignment inbox.
