## Positioning lines wired

- Toast: *Hardware shouldn't lock you in.*
- Square: *Enterprise features without enterprise contracts.*
- MarketMan: *Full OS — including marketplace.*
- MarginEdge: *Invoice AI is a feature, not a product.*
- R365: kitchen-ops-first alternative framing (no separate positioning section)

## Honesty

Each page includes competitor **wins**, **verify** pricing before switch, **not affiliated** disclaimers, and **BETA** limitation bullets — typical 2026 positioning, no fake LIVE badges.
