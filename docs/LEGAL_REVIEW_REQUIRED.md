# Legal review required

OS Kitchen legal pages are starter templates. Counsel must review before broad commercial use.

Review required for:
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- DPA
- Cookie policy
- Acceptable use policy
- Data retention and deletion process
- Tax setting disclaimers
- Nutrition/allergen disclaimers
- Delivery platform availability wording
- Integration credential responsibility

Required disclaimers:
- Nutrition and allergen data must be verified by the business.
- Taxes must be verified by the business.
- Uber Eats requires appropriate partner/API access.
- Shopify/WooCommerce require proper credentials.
- Legal templates are not legal advice.
