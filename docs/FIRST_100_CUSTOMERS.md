# First 100 customers playbook

## ICP (initial)

- **Geo**: English-speaking US/CA/EU metros with strong delivery culture.
- **Segments**: Weekly meal prep (10–500 meals/week), boutique catering with preorder, single-location ghost kitchens testing 2–4 brands.
- **Tech**: Already on WooCommerce or Shopify for preorder; tolerates light ops tooling.

## Buyer persona

- **Economic buyer**: Owner or COO cares about margin and labor hours.
- **Champion**: Kitchen manager or ops lead drowning in spreadsheets/slack threads.
- **User**: Line cooks + packers need simple checkpoints.

## Acquisition loops

1. **Demo-first**: `/demo` → signup → onboarding → live channel connect.
2. **Content**: “Replace spreadsheet production” SEO + LinkedIn teardown posts.
3. **Agency**: Small Shopify meal-prep studios bundle KitchenOS as fulfillment OS.

## Success milestones

- 10 paying kitchens with weekly active Order hub usage.
- 3 written testimonials (even if logo-blind).
- One integration co-marketing post (Shopify or Woo partner).

## Retention hooks

- Weekly email summary: orders by channel, packing completion %.
- Alerts on failed webhooks / unmatched SKUs (once notification center ships).

## Expansion

- Add location #2 upsell after stable single-site weekly usage.
