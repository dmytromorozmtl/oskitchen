# OS Kitchen — 5-minute demo video script

**Policy:** `demo-video-script-5min-mkt12-v1`  
**Updated:** 2026-06-03  
**Duration:** **5:00** (±10s)  
**Format:** Screen recording + voiceover · 1920×1080  
**Workspace:** Staging golden scenario (`/demo/meal-prep` import) or `/demo` → owner login  
**Audience:** ICP prospects (ghost kitchen, meal prep, weekly preorder) · LinkedIn · website hero  
**Honesty:** [`sales-safe-claims-registry.md`](./sales-safe-claims-registry.md) · [`forbidden-claims-training.md`](./forbidden-claims-training.md)  
**Companion:** [`demo-video-script-today.md`](./demo-video-script-today.md) (90s Today cut-down)

Record as **one continuous take** with chapter markers, or edit from segment B-roll. Do not imply signed customers, LIVE marketplace integrations, or audited ROI.

## Script by segment

### 0:00–0:30 — Hook (30s)

**VO:**  
Restaurant operators juggle five tabs before the first ticket prints — orders in one app, kitchen on paper, channels in another dashboard, margins in a spreadsheet. OS Kitchen is one operating system for **order-to-fulfillment**: storefront, POS, kitchen, packing, and channel health in a single workspace. We're in **pilot** — integrations are labeled honestly, not marketed as finished. Let me show you a real owner day.

**On screen:**  
Open `os-kitchen.com` hero (3s) → **Start free trial** hover → cut to logged-in **Today** (`/dashboard/today`). Business-mode badge visible.

**Do not say:** "Replace Toast overnight," "fully integrated," "proven ROI."

---

### 0:30–1:15 — Today Command Center (45s)

**VO:**  
Every day starts on **Today**. Your **daily briefing** pulls workspace signals — priorities, integration risk, next actions — not generic AI fluff. Below that, **attention** surfaces blockers before your team feels them. Orders due, open tasks, routes — each row deep-links to the fix. When the shift is busy, the KPI wall expands; when it's calm, Today stays quiet.

**On screen:**  
Pan **Owner Daily Briefing** hero (8s). Scroll **attention strip** or calm card. Hover 2 KPI tiles. Quick jump hover on **Kitchen screen**.

**Alt (quiet demo):** Use "Everything is calm today" card → **Create order** (5s).

---

### 1:15–2:00 — Order Hub + Integration Health (45s)

**VO:**  
**Order Hub** is the spine — every channel, walk-in, and storefront order in one queue. Filter by status, channel, or fulfillment date. Drill into an order: customer, line items, production state. This is where channel chaos ends — but we're honest about **what's live**. **Integration Health** scores connected channels; BETA and SKIPPED mean we haven't certified live proof yet. Green configured is not the same as smoke-certified PASS.

**On screen:**  
Navigate **Orders** → open one order (10s). Jump to **Integration Health** strip or `/dashboard/integration-health`. Pause on one **BETA** or **degraded** badge — point cursor, do not dismiss modal.

---

### 2:00–2:45 — Kitchen + KDS (45s)

**VO:**  
**Kitchen** is built for line speed — tickets by station, bump to advance, optional sound. Our KDS refreshes on a safety interval with a visible **connection bar** — we're qualified for pilot kitchens, not rush-hour certified yet. From here, production flows to packing and routes without re-keying.

**On screen:**  
Open **Kitchen screen** / KDS. Show 2–3 tickets. **Bump** one ticket (if safe in demo). Highlight connection bar / LIVE vs polling label (5s).

**Do not say:** "Sub-second realtime," "Toast-class KDS certification."

---

### 2:45–3:30 — Software-first POS (45s)

**VO:**  
**POS** runs on the devices you already own — iPad, browser, handheld. No proprietary terminal lock-in. Quick tiles for high-volume items, tabs, and card checkout in **test mode** on staging. Offline card queue is engineering preview — we say that on the limitation sheet, not in fine print.

**On screen:**  
`/dashboard/pos` → add item to cart → show total → **Pay** flow (test card). Flash handheld layout if time (optional 5s cut).

---

### 3:30–4:15 — Channels + storefront (45s)

**VO:**  
Connect **WooCommerce** or **Shopify** with our five-step pilot wizard — credentials, test connection, webhooks, verification. Native **storefront** and **QR guest ordering** are BETA — great for design partners shaping the roadmap. Marketplace B2B supply is a separate pilot surface. We screen-share Integration Health so buyers never discover a SKIPPED row in week three.

**On screen:**  
**Sales channels** overview (8s) → flash Woo/Shopify setup wizard (8s) → **Storefront** settings or public menu preview (10s). Return to Integration Health thumbnail in sidebar if visible.

---

### 4:15–5:00 — Profit + close (45s)

**VO:**  
**Profit** and food-cost views tie recipe costing to margin alerts — owner-facing, BETA accuracy. We're recruiting **founding design partners** for a three-month pilot: dedicated workspace, weekly feedback, honest integration labels. Start a free trial or book a walkthrough — link on screen. OS Kitchen: one OS, honest maturity, pilot-ready infrastructure.

**On screen:**  
Open **Profit** or food-cost dashboard (10s). Return to **Today** briefing hero (5s). End card: **os-kitchen.com/book-demo** · **/beta** · logo. Lower third: *7 AI modules · BETA integrations · Pilot program open*
