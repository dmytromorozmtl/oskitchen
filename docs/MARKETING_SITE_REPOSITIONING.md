# Marketing site repositioning

## Positioning headline

**“The operating system for modern food operations.”**

## Route inventory (examples)

- Homepage: `app/page.tsx` (verify), pricing `app/pricing/page.tsx`, resources `app/resources/*`, help `app/help/*`.

## SEO targets (clusters)

Food operations software, kitchen production software, meal prep software, catering software, ghost kitchen software, preorder management, packing labels, delivery route planning — **unique landing copy per cluster** to avoid thin duplicate pages.

## CTA ladder

Start trial, Book demo, Explore demo, Join beta, Contact sales — each must resolve to a **working** form or mailto with analytics events.

## Priority

**P1** — conversion is part of “market ready”.
